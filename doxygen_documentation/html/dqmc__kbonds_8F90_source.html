119  ! construct bonds amongst states (iat,k) and (jat,k')

121  ! requested one. Note that a bond has a direction:
122  ! it goes from (iat,k), where an up spin particle is
123  ! created, to (jat,k), where a down one is created.
124  ! It is possible for some of the pairs to be identical
125  ! (overcounting). This, however, does not pose any major
126  ! problem apart from a small overhead during the computation
